WHY CHOOSE IMC

  • Personal assistance and care – our staff will help you with pre visit planning so that you can manage your time in the best possible way, we schedule medical appointments, meet and accompany you to doctors’ consultations
  • Personal phone number – we provide organizational support & healthcare consulting
  • Optimal registration process – we’ll help you save your time and get things done
  • VIP zone – comfortable waiting room, cozy seatings, refreshments, art and reading materials for you
  • Client engagement – our consultants know how to help you. We offer help at the National Medical Centre in Tashkent, at branches of the National Medical Centre throughout Uzbekistan and a home visit service. We guarantee medical confidentiality
  • Communication - we make sure you get the results of outpatient examinations, laboratory tests, and medical records via e-mail or WhatsApp, keep your personal appointment schedule, schedule you for future appointments, arrange for pre-visit lab testing, gather all the necessary information for upcoming visits, update you on promotions and current NMC Check-Up programs
